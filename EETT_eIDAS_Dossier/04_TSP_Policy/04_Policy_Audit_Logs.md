## Purpose
This document maintains a log of internal audits, reviews, and changes related to the TSP Policy (as defined in `04_TSP_Policy_EN.md` and related files). It ensures traceability and compliance with eIDAS Art. 24(2), ETSI EN 319 401 Clause 6 (policy requirements), and internal change management processes. Logs are updated quarterly or upon significant events.

Entries include:  
- **Date:** Timestamp of the audit/action.  
- **Auditor/Reviewer:** Responsible individual or team.  
- **Action Type:** e.g., Review, Change, Compliance Check.  
- **Details:** Description of the activity.  
- **Outcome/Status:** Results, any issues, and resolutions.  
- **References:** Links to related documents or tickets.
