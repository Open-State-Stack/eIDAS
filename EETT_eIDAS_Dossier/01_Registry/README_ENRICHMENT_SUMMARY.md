## USAGE INSTRUCTIONS

### For First-Time Submission (New TSP)

1. **Read** `01_REGISTRY_PLACEHOLDERS.md` to understand all placeholders
2. **Gather Data:** Collect all company info, insurance docs, director IDs
3. **Fill Template:** Replace all 72 placeholders with actual values
4. **Make Payment:** Transfer €300 to EETT (get IBAN from finance@eett.gr first)
5. **Attach Proof:** Scan bank receipt as PDF, attach to application
6. **Final Check:** Use submission checklist in each document's front matter
7. **Sign & Submit:** Apply qualified e-signature (or physical signature) and submit via EETT portal

**Timeline:** Allow 2-3 hours for data gathering + replacement

### For Change Notifications (Existing TSP)

1. **Identify Change:** What changed? (name, address, contact, service, etc.)
2. **Check Deadline:** Calculate 7 days from change date
3. **Open** `01b_Change_Notice_Template.md`
4. **Complete Section C:** Fill only the relevant subsection (§1-7)
5. **Track Progress:** Use 7-day tracking table to ensure compliance
6. **Attach Supporting Docs:** GEMI update, director IDs, etc. as applicable
7. **Submit:** Email to [EETT_TRUST_SERVICES_EMAIL] or via EETT portal
8. **Update Website:** Don't forget to update your public website too!

**Timeline:** Can be completed in 30 minutes if all documents ready
